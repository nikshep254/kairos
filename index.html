What if you could feel time passing,
not just track it?

The Intention
Most apps make time feel abstract.
Numbers you swipe away.

This is different.

Each card breathes with the clock. You see the percentage of your hour, your day, your year slipping past — and it changes how you spend the next moment.

That awareness, Nikshep believed, is the most powerful motivator there is. Not guilt. Not urgency. Just presence.
