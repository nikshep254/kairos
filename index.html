The Intention
Most apps make time feel abstract. Numbers you swipe away.

KairosFlow is different.

Each card breathes with the clock. You see your hour, your day, your year slipping past — and it changes how you spend the next moment.

That awareness is the most powerful motivator there is. Not guilt. Not urgency. Just presence.
